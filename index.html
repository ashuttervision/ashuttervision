Instagram feed

Instagram doesn't allow websites to pull your grid directly without an approved API token, so this uses a free feed service as the bridge. One-time setup (~5 min):
1. Go to behold.so and sign in with the Instagram account.
2. Create a feed, pick JSON feed (not widget), set it to your latest posts (up to 50).
3. Copy the feed URL it gives you and paste it below.
The site then renders your posts as a clean image grid — no Instagram branding, no embeds, just your shots, each linking to its post. New posts show up automatically.
